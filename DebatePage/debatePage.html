Who?

We are the Debate Club, one of the 35+ clubs present in DA-IICT, Gandhinagar.

What?

A group of individuals that gets together for intellectual (sometimes heated ) discussions.

How?

  • Debate - Asian and British Parliamentary Debate
  • Model United Nations
  • Group discussions
  • Turncoats
  • Just a Minute (JAM)

Why?

We believe engaging in debate's helps a person build his personality. It imparts you with confidence, public speaking skills and the ability to think about an argument from both ends.
